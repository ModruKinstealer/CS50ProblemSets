About Me

This page was created as part of the 2022 CS50 course by Harvard through the EdX platform

My first foray in to programming was typing out a program from a Computer magazine. Later in juniour high we had a quarter where we learned a bit about Basic. Back when the internet was still young I used to write and edit macros in mIRC for ASCII art and other things. Then some years later I wrote macros for Maptools, a open source VTT program for the TTRP Dungeons and Dragons. I got interested in trying out programming in a lot of ways to make writing mods and macros for VTTs easier. I took a course, MIT's introduction to computer science and programming, but with other things going on I have fallen out of practice. So I decided to give it another try with CS50 with the goal of transitioning careers into a development position. I decided on the CS50 route due to it's reviews and more general scope of the course.

My hobbies include Dungeons and Dragons, reading, watching movies, playing video games, and woodworking.
